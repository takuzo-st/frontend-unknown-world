    • Book review: ”Thinking map of Emmanuel Todd”

      Many people hope they can predict their future. I first knew a researcher who thought a predicting process. He’s Emmanuel Todd who is a French historian, anthropologist, demographer, sociologist and scientist. He publised “The final fall” and “The Diversity Of The World” and so on. this time, I introduce his predicting process written in ”Thinking map of Emmanuel Todd.”

      The predicting process which he mentioned is divided 3 phases. First, he’s based on the empiricism, he collect numerous data. He makes facts for the importanc because he’s a empiricism of the Anglo-Saxon. Second, He conpares the data and his individual experience, history to confirm whether the data is valid and not to dissociate among them. Third, he predict artistically by the exercise of imagination from second phase. Those 3 phases are a predicting process.

    To take an example, He went to Hungary which is a calm society in socialist countries but it was lesser developed and a nomal conutry. This fact is first phase of his predicting process. He has rgarded the communism as something great, so he accepted the fact which was difference compared to his imagination. This is a second phase. He says accepting abnomal things is insight. On third phase, he reached on one hypothesis that the Soviet Union will collapse. He says it need an original idea to have artistic aspects on the third phace. People who have an efficent brain can’t generate a new idea. Conversely, it is important to go abroad or to watch Sci-Fi to generate a unique idea by giving stimulation to the brain. The external stimulation is important to get out from thinkismend to go a nice place to think and try new things. Thank you for reading until the end.
